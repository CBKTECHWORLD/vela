Our Story

The Taste of Madurai, Now in Coimbatore

VELA brings you the raw, smoky, unapologetically bold flavours of authentic Madurai street-side non-veg cooking — right here in Kovaipudur. Every dish is crafted from secret family recipes passed down through generations, slow-cooked with freshly ground spices on wood fire, just like the legendary dhabas of Madurai that started it all.
